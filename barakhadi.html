Barakhadi - Hindi Vowels (स्वर)

Master all 14 Hindi vowels with detailed pronunciation guides

1 of 14
अ
a
"u" in "but"

Mouth Position

Tongue: Neutral, flat, middle - not touching roof of mouth
Lips: Neutral - not rounded, not spread
Jaw: Relaxed, slightly open (1-2 finger widths)
Throat: Open, vocal cords vibrate

Practice

अब ab now
अगर agar if

Key Points

  • Most neutral vowel sound in Hindi
  • Short duration (unlike आ)
  • No tension in any part of mouth
  • Like the 'u' in British "but"
आ
aa
"a" in "father"

Mouth Position

Tongue: Low, pulled back
Lips: Neutral, wide opening
Jaw: Wide open (maximum opening)
Duration: Long vowel (twice as long as अ)

Practice

आम aam mango
आप aap you

Key Points

  • Long vowel - hold for twice the duration of अ
  • Maximum jaw opening
  • Clear, open sound
  • Very common in Hindi words
इ
i
"i" in "bit"

Mouth Position

Tongue: Front high position
Lips: Spread (slight smile)
Jaw: Nearly closed
Feature: Front + high tongue position

Practice

इस is this
किताब kitaab book

Key Points

  • Tongue moves to front-high position
  • Natural slight smile with lips
  • Short vowel duration
  • Clear, crisp sound

Memory Tricks

अ vs आ: Short "uh" vs Long "aah"
इ vs ई: "bit" vs "beat"
उ vs ऊ: "put" vs "pool"
ए vs ऐ: "day" (slight) vs "ice" (full glide)
